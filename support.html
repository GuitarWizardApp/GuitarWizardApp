FAQ

How do I set the tonic?
Double‑tap a note to set it as the tonic (home base).
How do I transpose?
Drag the transpose control to shift everything by semitones.
How do I practice a small area?
Long‑press any note on the fretboard to highlight a practice sector around it.
What does long‑press do in the app?
Long‑press actions:
  • "CAST YOUR SPELL!" → open the scale library.
  • "Info" → hide/show the interval panel (interval strip).
  • "Sound" → hide/show interval strip.
  • "Alakazam!" → open the guitar tuning menu.
  • Any fretboard note → highlight a practice sector.
